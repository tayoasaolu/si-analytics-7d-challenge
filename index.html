DAY 1

🏃 Physical Activity Dashboard

WHO recommendations, BMI, sedentary behavior

Python Plotly scikit-learn K-Means scipy
Analyzed daily activity patterns across age groups, sex, and BMI. Applied K-Means clustering for health risk segmentation. Only 35% meet WHO activity targets.
DAY 2

💵 U.S. State Finances Dashboard

Government spending, debt, and revenue (1992–2019)

Python Plotly scikit-learn Regression Choropleth
Explored 27 years of fiscal data across 50 states. K-Means clustering reveals 3 distinct fiscal profiles. Education accounts for ~30% of all state spending.
DAY 3

🩺 Pediatric HIV Care Dashboard

Python Plotly K-Means Chi-Square
Analyzed pediatric HIV survey data. K-Means clustering identifies 3 care quality profiles across health areas.
DAY 4

💹 Investment Habits During COVID-19

Risk, asset preferences & financial behavior during uncertainty

Python Plotly K-Means Chi-Square
